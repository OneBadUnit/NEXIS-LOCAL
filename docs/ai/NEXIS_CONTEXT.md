\# NEXIS CONTEXT



\## Product Identity



NEXIS is a local-first AI-assisted information workflow platform built under ARC NEXUS.



Core workflow:

1\. Collect

2\. Select

3\. Create

4\. Refine

5\. Reuse



NEXIS helps users transform raw information into structured, reusable outputs.



\---



\## Product Philosophy



NEXIS prioritizes:

\- practical workflows

\- understandable UX

\- maintainable systems

\- controllable AI integration

\- local-first flexibility



NEXIS is intended to feel:

\- workflow-oriented

\- readable

\- stable

\- creator-friendly

\- trustworthy



Avoid:

\- unnecessary complexity

\- enterprise-style abstraction

\- confusing workflows

\- excessive UI clutter
